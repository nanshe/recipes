Ingredients:
 - bag of blue tortilla chips
 - can of Amy's refried beans
 - baby spinach
 - 8 oz shredded cheese
 - jar of Newman's salsa

Directions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 375
2. Empty bag of tortilla chips onto baking sheet
3. Spoon refried beans onto chips
4. Add handfuls of baby spinach to chips and beans
5. Sprinkle shredded cheese over chips, beans, and spinach
6. Bake for 10 minutes
7. Dish out nachos and add salsa
